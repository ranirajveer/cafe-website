About
At Sweet Treats Cafe, we believe that coffee and desserts are the perfect match. That's why we're passionate about creating the best coffee and dessert experience for our customers. Our coffee is locally sourced and roasted, and we offer a variety of brewing methods, including pour-over, French press, and espresso. Our desserts are made fresh daily, with high-quality ingredients and no preservatives. We offer a wide range of sweet treats, including cakes, pastries, cookies, and ice cream. We also have vegan and gluten-free options available. Our cozy cafe is the perfect place to relax and enjoy a cup of coffee and a sweet treat. Come visit us and indulge in the perfect pairing of coffee and dessert!
